About FearMeGame

FearMeGame is a fan-created knowledge hub dedicated to Fear Me: Jeff the Killer's Dating Sim. Our mission is to help players survive Jeff's psychological mind games by providing accurate guides, endings breakdowns, and updates from the developer Neesa.

We compile official announcements, community discoveries, and strategic walkthroughs so newcomers and veteran fans can make informed choices and unlock every ending. This site is not affiliated with Neesa Complex, but we credit and link back to the official itch.io release at all times.
